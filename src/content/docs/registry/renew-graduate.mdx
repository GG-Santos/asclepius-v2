title: Renew a Graduate

Last reviewed: 2026-06-12.

Renewal extends an active graduate license by the organization validity policy.
The current expiry becomes the latest re-certification date, and the new expiry
is calculated from the previous expiry.

<OpenRoute href="/dashboard/graduates">Open Graduates</OpenRoute>
<OpenRoute href="/dashboard/graduates/recertified">Open Recertified</OpenRoute>

## Where Renewal Appears

| Surface | Control |
| --- | --- |
| Graduates table | Calendar-plus row action, shown for active graduate records. |
| Graduate detail | **Renew +N yr** action beside Edit, Archive, Public credential, and Delete. |
| Recertified view | Renewed records appear in the selected date window for ZIP export. |

## Steps

<Steps>

<Step>

### Find the graduate

Open Graduates and search by exact LCN or name. Confirm the record is active
and belongs to the person being renewed.

</Step>

<Step>

### Start renewal

Choose **Renew** from the row action or **Renew +N yr** on the detail page. The
confirmation dialog explains that the current expiry becomes the latest
re-certification date.

</Step>

<Step>

### Confirm

Choose **Renew +N yr** in the dialog only when renewal evidence is complete.

</Step>

<Step>

### Verify dates

Reopen the graduate detail page and public credential. Confirm latest
re-certification and expiration dates.

</Step>

<Step>

### Export renewed IDs

Open Recertified, choose the date window, then download the ZIP when cards are
ready for print review.

</Step>

</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Cause | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Renew action is missing | The record is not an active graduate. | Check status and archive state before renewal. |
| Recertified view is empty | No renewals happened in the selected window. | Switch to 1d, 7d, 30d, 90d, or use a custom date range. |
| New expiry is not what you expected | Renewal extends from current expiry, not from today's date. | Inspect prior expiry and org validity policy. |
| ZIP reports failed cards | Artifact rendering failed for one or more records. | Do not distribute the ZIP until each failure is reviewed. |

<DocNote title="Renewal is evidence-backed" tone="warn">
Do not renew to make a record look valid. Renew only after the official renewal
or re-certification evidence exists.
</DocNote>

Last reviewed: 2026-06-12.

Registry is the primary production workflow in WSL EMS. It owns the records that
move a learner from a training batch to a public credential. Courses, Publishing,
and Administration are still experimental or under active development; use those
sections carefully, but rely on Registry as the current operating source of
truth.

## Operating Model

| Owner | Route | What it controls |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Batches | `/dashboard/batches` | Cohort code, name, professor, media, grading setup, roster, and graduation action. |
| Students | `/dashboard/students` | In-training identity, batch assignment, photo, raw quiz scores, practical scores, and training status. |
| Graduates | `/dashboard/graduates` | Public credential truth, LCN, expiry, renewal, archive state, cards, certificates, and portal account link. |
| Verification | `/verify/[lcn]` | Public view of one credential. Always verify here after public-facing edits. |

## Registry Sequence

<Steps>

<Step>

### Set up the batch

Create the batch before adding learners. The batch is the cohort anchor for
assignment, grading setup, graduation, public cohort pages, and generated LCNs.

</Step>

<Step>

### Add students

Student records hold training data only. They do not have LCNs. Complete the
profile, batch assignment, photo, quiz scores, and practical scores before
graduation.

</Step>

<Step>

### Graduate the batch

Use the batch graduation dialog when the roster is complete. Students at 70% or
above become graduate records. Students below 70% are withdrawn. Missing scores
block the action.

</Step>

<Step>

### Maintain the graduate registry

After graduation, the graduate record becomes the source of truth for public
verification, expiry, renewal, artifacts, ranking, and portal access.

</Step>

</Steps>

Last reviewed: 2026-06-12.

Credential downloads live on the graduate detail page. Admins can open the
certificate and identity dialogs, toggle signature layers, flip ID sides, and
download print-grade PNG files.

<OpenRoute href="/dashboard/graduates">Open Graduates</OpenRoute>

<WorkflowMedia title="Download Credentials" slug="download-credentials" />

## Available Exports

| Artifact | Where | Button |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Certificate | **View Certificate** dialog | **Download PNG** |
| ID front | **View Identity** dialog, front side visible | **Download front PNG** |
| ID back | **View Identity** dialog after **Show back** | **Download back PNG** |
| Renewed card bundle | Recertified view | **Download ZIP (n card images)** |

## Steps

<Steps>

<Step>

### Open the graduate detail page

From Graduates, search for the record and choose **View record**.

</Step>

<Step>

### Review the credential first

Check name, LCN, dates, status, photo, scores, and public credential link before
exporting artifacts.

</Step>

<Step>

### Download the certificate

Choose **View Certificate**. Toggle signature layers only when required, then
choose **Download PNG**.

</Step>

<Step>

### Download ID card sides

Choose **View Identity**. Download the front, then choose **Show back** and
download the back.

</Step>

<Step>

### Review the exported files

Open the PNG files before printing or sharing. Check text fit, signature state,
QR visibility, date labels, and photo quality.

</Step>

</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Cause | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Download button says Rendering | The artifact is being rasterized. | Wait for the browser download to finish. |
| `Nothing to render yet` | The dialog content has not mounted or a side is hidden. | Reopen the dialog or switch to the side you want to download. |
| Download fails after photo change | The image source needs a reload before canvas export. | Reload the graduate detail page and try again. |
| QR or text is hard to read | Template, long text, or export review issue. | Check template settings and graduate field lengths before distribution. |

<DocNote title="Downloads are print files, not proof by themselves" tone="info">
The public verification route remains the live source. Review `/verify/[lcn]`
alongside any downloaded artifact.
</DocNote>

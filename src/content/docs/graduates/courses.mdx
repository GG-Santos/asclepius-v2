The portal hosts continuing-education (CE) courses for WSL EMS graduates.

## Taking a course

Open a course to see its modules. Each module holds items — reading pages,
videos, downloadable resources, links, and graded quizzes. Work through them in
order; some modules stay locked until you finish the ones before them, and some
items unlock only once you complete the item above them. Your progress is saved
as you go, so you can stop and pick up where you left off.

## Quizzes

Some items are quizzes you must **pass** (the pass mark is shown on the quiz).
Quizzes are graded instantly. Unless the quiz limits attempts, you can retake it
until you pass.

## Earning a certificate

When you complete every required item in a course — including passing its
quizzes — the portal issues a **CE certificate** you can download.

<Callout type="info" title="A CE certificate is not your license">
A CE certificate records continuing education you've completed. It's separate
from the certificate of completion and license card shown on your
[verification page](/docs/verify) — those represent your original credential.
</Callout>

The portal hosts continuing-education (CE) courses for WSL EMS graduates.

## Taking a course

Open a course to see its lessons, then work through them in order. Your progress
is saved as you go, so you can stop and pick up where you left off.

## Earning a certificate

When you complete a course, the portal issues a **CE certificate** you can
download.

<Callout type="info" title="A CE certificate is not your license">
A CE certificate records continuing education you've completed. It's separate
from the certificate of completion and license card shown on your
[verification page](/docs/verify) — those represent your original credential.
</Callout>

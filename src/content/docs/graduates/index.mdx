---
title: Claim your portal account
description: Graduates — register for the portal to take CE courses and download certificates.
---

If you trained and were certified at WSL EMS, you already have a record in the
registry. Claim a portal account to manage your continuing education.

## What you'll need

- Your **License Number (LCN)**.
- Your **last name**, exactly as it appears on your certificate.
- An email address and a password (at least 8 characters).

## Register

<Steps>

<Step>

### Open the portal sign-in

Reach it from the home page, then choose to register a new account.

</Step>

<Step>

### Match your record

Enter your LCN and last name. They must match your registry record — this is how
the portal confirms the account belongs to you.

</Step>

<Step>

### Set your email and password

Add an email and a password, and you're in.

</Step>

</Steps>

<Callout type="warn" title="If your name won't match">
Registration matches the **last name** on your record. Older (Legacy) records
sometimes store only a single full name — enter your last name as printed on your
certificate. Still stuck? See [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting).
</Callout>

## Inside the portal

- **CE courses** — work through lessons at your own pace.
- **CE certificates** — download a certificate when you complete a course.
- **Your credential** — view your own registry details.

See [CE courses & certificates](/docs/graduates/courses) for how courses work.

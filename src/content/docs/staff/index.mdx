Staff sign in from the home page to reach the dashboard — the back office for the
registry.

## What you can manage

- **Students** — people currently in training.
- **Graduates** — certified EMTs published to the public registry.
- **Batches** — the cohort each record belongs to.
- **Courses** — continuing-education courses for the portal.
- **Blog** — public posts.

## Day to day

- Add and edit student and graduate records, including photos.
- Promote students to graduates when they're certified — see [Students,
  promotion & batches](/docs/staff/records).
- Manage CE course content for graduates.

<Callout type="info" title="Staff edits are public immediately">
Publishing or editing a graduate updates what the public sees on that
credential's [verification page](/docs/verify) right away.
</Callout>

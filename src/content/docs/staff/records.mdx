## Students vs graduates

A **student** is in training and not in the public registry. A **graduate** is
certified, appears on the public site, and is verifiable by LCN.

## Promotion

Promoting a student to graduate issues their credential, computes their ranking
(per batch and overall), and publishes the record to the registry.

## Batches and Legacy

Every record belongs to a **batch**, and Legacy status is assigned automatically
from the batch number:

- **Batch 16 and below → Legacy.** Scores were entered manually, before
  automated scoring existed.
- **Batch 17 and above.** Scores are computed automatically through the
  promotion flow.

Legacy affects only how proficiency scores were recorded — never a credential's
validity. See [Credential statuses](/docs/verify/statuses).

Last reviewed: 2026-06-12.

Settings are operational controls. Change one thing at a time and verify the
surface it affects.

## Staff access

| Role | Intended user | Access summary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Admin | Operations owner or trusted manager. | Full dashboard, wiki, registry, courses, content, staff, and settings. |
| Professor | Instructor assigned to active cohorts. | Limited dashboard access for assigned in-training batches. |
| Graduate | Certified EMT using the portal. | Portal access tied to one graduate LCN. |

## Staff workflow

<OpenRoute href="/dashboard/staff">Open Staff</OpenRoute>

1. Open Staff.
2. Use "Add staff" for new admin or professor accounts.
3. For existing accounts, edit Name, Email, and Role from the staff table.
4. Use password reset only for the intended account.
5. Do not change your own role as a workaround for access testing.

## Expiry policy

<OpenRoute href="/dashboard/settings/expiry">Open Expiry Policy</OpenRoute>

| Field | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| License validity (years) | Default duration added to graduation or renewal dates. |
| Auto-archive grace (years) | How long an expired graduate can remain before automatic archiving. |

After saving, open a graduate record and a public verification page to confirm
the dates and state labels behave as expected.

## Artifact templates

<OpenRoute href="/dashboard/settings/templates">Open Templates</OpenRoute>

The page has Certificate, ID - front, and ID - back tabs. Each template can
change text, record text colors, artwork layers, and the live sample preview.

Review every template change against:

- Public wording and center name.
- Signature text and visual placement.
- Date and renewal language.
- Long graduate names.
- Card and certificate text fit.

## Change workflow

<Steps>

<Step>

### Confirm the reason

Before changing expiry, templates, or staff roles, write down the reason in the
ticket, message, or operational note.

</Step>

<Step>

### Make one change

Change one policy or template at a time. This makes it easier to identify the
cause if artifacts, access, or portal behavior changes.

</Step>

<Step>

### Verify the affected surface

For expiry changes, inspect a graduate record. For template changes, preview
license cards and certificates. For role changes, confirm the account lands in
the expected dashboard or portal area.

</Step>

</Steps>

<DocNote title="Access follows least privilege" tone="info">
If someone only teaches a cohort, use professor. If someone only needs their own
records and courses, use graduate. Reserve admin for operations owners.
</DocNote>

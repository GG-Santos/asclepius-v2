---
title: Verification links & QR codes
description: Share or print a credential with a permanent link or QR code.
---

Every verification result has a stable address you can share, bookmark, or print.

## Permanent link

Each credential resolves at a fixed path: `/verify/<LCN>` — for example,
`/verify/A09-240801`. The link always points at the live registry, so it stays
accurate as a credential is renewed or expires.

## QR code

Every result page shows a QR code that encodes its verification link. Scan it to
open the same verification page on another device — useful when an EMT's card or
certificate carries the code and someone wants to confirm it on the spot.

## For employers and verifiers

The link *is* the proof. To confirm a credential, open its verification page and
read the status — no account required, and you can re-check the same link any
time to see the credential's current state.

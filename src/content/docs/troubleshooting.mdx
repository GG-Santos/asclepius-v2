---
title: Troubleshooting
description: Common issues with verifying a credential or claiming a portal account.
---

## "No active credential" for a number I know is valid

LCNs are matched exactly. Confirm the prefix, the hyphen, and the digits
(`A09-240801`), and that capitalization matches what's printed. Worn cards often
blur `0`/`O` and `1`/`I`. If it still doesn't resolve, the credential may predate
the registry — contact WSL EMS.

## The profile shows no proficiency scores

It's a [Legacy](/docs/verify/statuses) record (batch 16 or earlier). Detailed
scores weren't digitized for those batches. The license is still valid.

## The result says "Expired"

The credential is past its valid-until date. The record is real, but the license
isn't currently active — renewal is handled by WSL EMS.

## I can't register for the portal

Registration matches the **last name** on your registry record. Legacy records
sometimes store only a single full name — enter your last name exactly as printed
on your certificate, and confirm your LCN. See [Claim your portal
account](/docs/graduates).

## There's no photo on the result

Not every record has a photo on file. It doesn't affect the credential's validity.

## I scanned a QR code and it didn't open

The QR encodes the credential's verification link (`/verify/<LCN>`). If it won't
open, type the LCN into the home page instead — see [Verify a
credential](/docs/verify).

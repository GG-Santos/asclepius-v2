## First Admin Pass

<Steps>

<Step>

### Open the overview

Review KPI cards, expiring credentials, recent verification lookups, and linked
alerts before editing records.

</Step>

<Step>

### Choose the record owner

Batches own cohort setup. Students own in-training data. Graduates own public
credential truth. Courses own learning content only.

</Step>

<Step>

### Make one change

Change one record, policy, or template at a time. This keeps verification clear
when a public page, portal page, or export changes.

</Step>

<Step>

### Verify the affected surface

After saving, open the affected admin record and the public or portal route that
uses it.

</Step>

</Steps>

<DocNote title="Admin actions can be public" tone="warn">
Graduation, graduate edits, renewals, artifact downloads, course publishing,
testimonials, team profiles, blog posts, and templates can change what visitors
or graduates see.
</DocNote>

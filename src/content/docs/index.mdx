## Verify a credential

<Steps>

<Step>

### Find the License Number

It's printed on the EMT's card or certificate, formatted like `A09-240801`.

</Step>

<Step>

### Enter it and press Verify

Open the [home page](/) and enter the LCN exactly as printed — surrounding spaces are ignored, but the rest is matched exactly.

</Step>

<Step>

### Read the result

A match shows the licensee's profile, proficiency record, license card, and certificate. Every result has a permanent link (`/verify/A09-240801`) and a QR code, so you can share it or re-scan it on any device.

</Step>

</Steps>

## What the statuses mean

<StatusReferenceTable />

<Callout type="info" title="Legacy doesn't mean invalid">
Legacy records (batch 16 or earlier) were graded before automated scoring, so detailed proficiency scores aren't on file. The license itself is valid — Legacy status does not affect validity.
</Callout>
